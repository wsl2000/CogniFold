DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Memory that understands you — not a search box.

CogniFold steps away from retrieval-based memory. Instead of fetching the right snippet on demand, it works the way human memory does — letting structure emerge on its own, managing its own active state, and growing intentions out of what it has come to understand.
